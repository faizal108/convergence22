Ganpat University Convergence 2022

Convergence 2022 is one of the most awaited events of the year, making campus vibrant, full of energy, excitement, and youthfulness. It is the combination of nine wings, namely - Infocrats, CIVESTA, Mech-Mechato, PetroX, Biotechnica, Electabuzz, MARITECH, MATHMAGIX, General Events. The Nine Wings encourages students from various field such as Technology, Computer Engineering, IT, Civil, Electrical Engineering and many more to showcase and test their skillset a national platform.
